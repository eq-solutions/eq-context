# 2026-06-05 — EQ Field: job numbers on the schedule (v3.5.73 + v3.5.74)

**Built by:** Royce Milmlow + Claude Code

## Shipped (both merged + live)
- **v3.5.73** — one job per *project*, derived onto the roster grid + My Schedule (PR #186).
- **v3.5.74** — per-cell job *pin* for multi-job sites (Edit Roster pick-list); resolution pin > project primary > none (PR #187).

## Why
Team asked to see job numbers on the weekly schedule. Started with the low-friction
project-level case (v3.5.73), then handled the harder one Royce flagged: a single site can
carry several job numbers (esp. service jobs), so the person attending needs to know which.

## Operating model captured (Royce)
The **Weekly Roster is the single surface every worker reads** (where + who with). Direct
staff book hours in **Workbench**; EQ Field timesheets are a *tracking/reconciliation* surface
(execs reconcile Field vs Workbench). **Labour-hire & apprentices** are entered into Field
timesheets so **accounts reconcile against their employers' invoices** (both are effectively
subcontractors). So the roster job number is the *communicated truth* direct workers carry
into Workbench — not something direct workers self-enter in Field.

## Decisions
- Job numbers originate in **Workbench** (live-sync out of scope) → Field's Job Numbers list is
  import-maintained (CSV import exists). site→jobs association = `job_numbers.site_name`.
- Cell job stored as the plain **number string** (matches `timesheets.{day}_job`; durable across
  Workbench re-imports where ids churn).
- Editor UX: progressive disclosure — picker appears **only** on multi-job-site cells; optional
  (blank = worker picks). Per-day granularity.
- **Deferred:** auto-fill LH/apprentice Field timesheets from the roster pin (money/reconciliation
  path) — its own future change.
